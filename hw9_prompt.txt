## Exercise
Study the stability of the system with transfer function

$H(z) = \dfrac{1}{z^2 - z + 0.5}$		

and determine its impulse response.

### Solution
The two poles are $p_{1,2} = \frac{1\pm i}{2}$ $|p_{1,2}| < 1$ $\rightarrow$ stable causal system (the unit circle is within the ROC which is $|z| > \text{max}(|p_1|, |p_2|)$)<img src="Meddins_zplane_4.png" width=500>
